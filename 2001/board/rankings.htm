Field Definitions:
 
Rank is an integer that identifies the position of the team in the overall standings.  For instance, a rank of 1 would be the Champion, 2 would be Second Place, 3 would be Third Place, and so forth.  If Rank is empty, the team will be identified as Honorable Mention.
 
Number Solved is the integer number of problems solved by the team.
 
Total Time is the integer number of minutes as defined in the contest rules.
 
Time of Last Solution is the integer number of minutes that elapsed from the beginning of the contest until the last problem was solved.
 
Citation overrides the citation that is calculated based on the value of Rank.
 
To simplify entry:
 
1. Alphabetize your standings list by institution and team name to match the order of the teams.
 
2. For each team, enter only the rank and then press the Update button.
 
3. When re-entering, the teams will be in rank order.  Enter the rest of the information from your ranked standings.
